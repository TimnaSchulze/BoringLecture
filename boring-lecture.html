Backyard Herbalism

The weeds in your backyard can keep you healthy!

Many of our weeds are invasive species brought from Europe as medicine during colonization.

Dandelions are great for digestion and your skin!

Clover helps clean your blood (red flowers) and lymph system (white flowers).

Plantain can clean your wounds and get rid of warts!

Rose Hips give you a healthy dose of vitamin C when you're feeling sick.

Don't kill your weeds. Use them to keep healthy!
